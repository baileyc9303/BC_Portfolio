# About this Project

As a student during COVID19, I struggled to focus on school during distance learning. I have noticed since that time, I have struggled more in classes than I even had before and wanted to do research into how the pandemic impacted students and if we will continue to see struggles from students in the future.

## What was done in this project?

For this project I had to find a dataset that contained information on the topic I chose. I also had to conduct my own smaller survey between my classmates. Some of the information was skewed, such as incorrect values, ages being above or below required limits and missing data. In order to get these datasets to work I first had to clean the data of any errors or broken limits by using if-elif or if-else statements. I also had to remove rows of missing values and fix type errors for things such as integers being shown as strings.

When collecting the data I used Google Forms, Kaggle for the dataset and Python for the programming aspect of the project.

## Challenges I faced:

I was struggling to get my own survey data to load in properly. Every time I attempted to import and use it, my Python would crash. I realized this was due to the file not downloading onto my laptop properly and I was able to redownload it and get it to work. On top of that, some of my visuals were creating errors that I had never seen. Pushing through it, I was able to figure out how to effectively show the trends and what I found through the datasets.
